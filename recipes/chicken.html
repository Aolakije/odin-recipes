Fried Chicken

This is an images of a well fried brownish nigerian chicken intended for jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps to make:

  1. Prepare the chicken by cleaning and seasoning it.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan.
  3. Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove and let it rest before serving.

Enjoy your delicious fried chicken!
