Fried Turkey

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey
  • Oil for frying
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps to make :

  1. Prepare the turkey by cleaning and seasoning it.
  2. Heat the oil in a large fryer.
  3. Carefully lower the turkey into the hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove and let it rest before serving.

Enjoy your delicious fried turkey!
